# 2.6.5 Accessing the Learning Community







You can access the Learning Community at [www.3ds.com/simulia](http://www.3ds.com/simulia) by selecting ****Help**![](../graphics/images/arrow.gif)**Learning Community****. The Learning Community contains online tutorials and technical content. The community also hosts a question-and-answer area that enables the global community of users to share their expertise and learn how to leverage the latest features and enhancements available in the SIMULIA portfolio.
