# 6.2 Eulerian elements with anisotropic materials







**Product: **Abaqus/Explicit  

**Benefits: **You can now perform a coupled Eulerian Lagrangian analysis with anisotropic materials. 

**Description: **For analyses involving extreme deformation, coupled Eulerian-Lagrangian analyses are more effective than traditional Lagrangian analyses because the elements do not deform and materials are allowed to flow through the element faces. This technology can now be used for models involving materials with anisotropic behavior.
