# *JOULE HEAT FRACTION







### *JOULE HEAT FRACTIONDefine the fraction of electric energy released as heat.

This option is used to specify the fraction of dissipated electrical energy released as heat in coupled thermal-electrical and coupled thermal-electrical-structural problems.

**Products: **Abaqus/Standard  Abaqus/CAE  

**Type: **Model data  

**Level: **Model  

**Abaqus/CAE: **Property module

- ["Coupled thermal-electrical analysis," Section 6.7.3 of the Abaqus Analysis User's Guide](../usb/usb-link.md#usb-anl-ajouleheating)
- ["Fully coupled thermal-electrical-structural analysis," Section 6.7.4 of the Abaqus Analysis User's Guide](../usb/usb-link.md#usb-anl-acoupthermalelecstruct)

**There are no parameters associated with this option.**
