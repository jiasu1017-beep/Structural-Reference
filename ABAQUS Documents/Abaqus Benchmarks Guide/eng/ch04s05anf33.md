# 4.5.8 Test 13R: Simply supported thin square plate: random forced vibration







**Product: **Abaqus/Standard  

### Elements tested

S4    S4R    S8R5    STRI65    

### Problem description

Material and geometry specifications are as given in ["Test 13: Simply supported thin square plate: frequency extraction," Section 4.5.5](ch04s05anf30.md).

**Loading: **

Uniform pressure of 1.0 applied to all elements, with white noise power spectral density of 10000 Pa2/Hz in the frequency region between 0 Hz and 1000 Hz, using the fully correlated type of random loading.

**Damping: **

16 modes with direct damping. ![](../graphics/bmk_eqn01725.gif) 2%.

**Response location: **

Peak PSD of displacement and stress at center of plate.

### Reference solution

This is a test recommended by the National Agency for Finite Element Methods and Standards (U.K.): Test 13R from NAFEMS “Selected Benchmarks for Forced Vibration,” R0016, September 1993.
