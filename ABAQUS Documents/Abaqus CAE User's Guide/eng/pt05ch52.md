# 52 Calculating linearized stresses







The calculation of linearized stresses is a capability for advanced Abaqus users. It is most commonly used for two-dimensional axisymmetric models. The following topics are covered:
- ["Understanding stress linearization," Section 52.1](pt05ch52s01.md)
- ["Stress linearization example," Section 52.2](pt05ch52s02.md)
- ["Obtaining linearized stress results," Section 52.3](pt05ch52hla01.md)

 For detailed information on linearized stress result quantities and the methods of calculation used, see ["Stress linearization," Section 2.17.1 of the Abaqus Theory Guide](../stm/stm-link.md#stm-anl-stresslinear).
