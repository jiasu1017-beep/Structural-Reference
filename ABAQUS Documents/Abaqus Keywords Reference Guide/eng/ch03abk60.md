# *CONTACT EXCLUSIONS







### *CONTACT EXCLUSIONSSpecify self-contact surfaces or surface pairings to exclude from the general contact domain.

This option is used to exclude self-contact surfaces and surface pairings from consideration by the general contact algorithm. It should be used in conjunction with the [*CONTACT](ch03abk54.md) option.

**Products: **Abaqus/Standard  Abaqus/Explicit  Abaqus/CAE  

**Type: **Model data in Abaqus/Standard; Model or history data in Abaqus/Explicit  

**Level: **Model in Abaqus/Standard; Model or Step in Abaqus/Explicit  

**Abaqus/CAE: **Interaction module
