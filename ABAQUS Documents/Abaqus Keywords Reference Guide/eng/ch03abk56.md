# *CONTACT CLEARANCE ASSIGNMENT







### *CONTACT CLEARANCE ASSIGNMENTAssign contact clearances between surfaces in the general contact domain.

This option is used to define initial contact clearances between contact surfaces and to control how initial contact overclosures are resolved in the general contact algorithm.

**Product: **Abaqus/Explicit  

**Type: **Model or history data  

**Level: **Model,  Step

##### **References:**

- ["Controlling initial contact status for general contact in Abaqus/Explicit," Section 36.4.4 of the Abaqus Analysis User's Guide](../usb/usb-link.md#usb-cni-aadjustgeneral)
- [*CONTACT](ch03abk54.md)
- [*CONTACT CLEARANCE](ch03abk55.md)

**There are no parameters associated with this option.**

### **Data lines to define nondefault contact clearances: **

**First line:**

Repeat this data line as often as necessary. If the contact clearance assignments overlap, the last assignment applies in the overlap region.
