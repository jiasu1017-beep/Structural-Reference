# 48 Viewing results along a path







A path is a series of connected lines that you define by specifying a series of points or edges through your model. You can view results along the path in the form of an *X–Y* plot. This chapter explains how to view results along a path. The following topics are covered:

- ["Creating a path through your model," Section 48.2](pt05ch48s02.md)
- ["Obtaining X--Y data along a path," Section 48.3](pt05ch48s03.md)
