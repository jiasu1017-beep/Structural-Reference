- ["Selecting field output variables," Section 54.3.2](pt05ch54s03hlb02.md)
- ["Selecting field output section points," Section 54.3.3](pt05ch54s03hlb03.md)
- ["Selecting options and thresholds for free body cut reports," Section 54.3.4](pt05ch54s03hlb04.md)

To learn how to control the contents of a tabular report of probe values, see [Chapter 51, "Probing the model](pt05ch51.md).”
