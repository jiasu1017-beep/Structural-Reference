# 48.2 Creating a path through your model







A path is a line you define by specifying a series of points or line segments through your model; the points can be nodes or coordinate locations, the line segments can be element edges or lines joining the ends of element edges. This section explains the following topics:

- ["Creating or editing a point list path," Section 48.2.2](pt05ch48s02hlb02.md)
- ["Creating or editing an edge list path," Section 48.2.3](pt05ch48s02hlb03.md)
- ["Creating or editing a circular path," Section 48.2.4](pt05ch48s02hlb04.md)
