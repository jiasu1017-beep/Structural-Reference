# 3.10.9 Pore-thermal model change







**Product: **Abaqus/Standard  

### Elements tested

C3D8PT    C3D8RPT    

### Features tested

Coupled pore-thermal elements are removed.

### Problem description

**Model: **

The models have dimensions 40.0  40 in the *x*–*y* plane, with an out-of-plane dimension of 5.0.

**Material: **

| Modulus | 88.7 109 |
| --- | --- |
| Density | 1922 |
| Expansion | 5.6 106 |
| Specific heat | 3000 |
| Conductivity | 3 |
| Density, pore fluid | 1000 |
| Expansion, pore fluid | 69. 106 |
| Specific heat, pore fluid | 4186 |
| Conductivity, pore fluid | 0.58 |
| Permeability | 0.001 |
| Specific weight of fluid | 9800 |

**Loading and boundary conditions: **

Pore pressure of 10 units and surface pressure of 2 units is applied on the top surface. The temperature at the top surface is set to 100 units. Normal displacement is constrained on three faces. Elements at the center of the block are removed and added in various steps.

### Results and discussion

The models produce results that match the expected values.
