# *PRESTRESS HOLD







### *PRESTRESS HOLDKeep rebar prestress constant during initial equilibrium solution.

This option is used within a [*STATIC](ch18abk31.md) step (["Static stress analysis," Section 6.2.2 of the Abaqus Analysis User's Guide](../usb/usb-link.md#usb-anl-astatic)) to keep the stress in some or all of the rebar constant during the initial equilibrium solution.

**Product: **Abaqus/Standard  

**Type: **History data 

**Level: **Step

##### **Reference:**

- ["Defining reinforcement," Section 2.2.3 of the Abaqus Analysis User's Guide](../usb/usb-link.md#usb-int-erebarlayer)

**There are no parameters associated with this option.**
