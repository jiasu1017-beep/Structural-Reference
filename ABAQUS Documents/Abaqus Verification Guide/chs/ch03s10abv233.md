### Features tested

Continuum acoustic elements are removed during a steady-state acoustic analysis.

### Problem description

**Model: **

The models have dimensions 10.0  1.0 in the *x*–*y* plane, with an out-of-plane dimension of 1.0.

**Material: **

| Bulk modulus, ![](../graphics/ver_eqn00378.gif) | 1.42176 105 |
| --- | --- |
| Density, ![](../graphics/ver_eqn01271.gif) | 1.293 |

**Loading and boundary conditions: **

The pressure at the left side of the model is constrained to equal one; there is a plane-wave radiation condition on the right side of the model. A steady-state solution at unit frequency is obtained. Then, one-half of the model is removed. When the elements are removed, the radiation condition is applied along the new external boundary.
